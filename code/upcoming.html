Maruti Suzuki Fronx

Rs.8.00 Lakh* - Rs.11.00 Lakh* Estimated Price in New Delhi

POWER SPEC:

maruti suzuki Fronx comes with 2 types of petrol engine options. The 1.2-litre K-Series Dual Jet, Dual VVT engine generates 89bhp at 6,000rpm and 113Nm of torque at 4,400rpm. This engine is available in both five-speed manual and five-speed AMT options. On the other hand, the 1.0-litre K-Series turbo petrol engine produces 99bhp at 5,500rpm and 147.6Nm of torque between 2,000 – 4,500rpm. The turbo engine is available in five-speed manual and six-speed automatic transmission options.

Fronx Safety:

On the safety front, the Fronx gets six airbags, ABD with EBD, ESP with hill hold assist and rollover mitigation, brake assist, and ISOFIX.

Fronx Variants:

The Fronx will be offered in five variants including Sigma, Delta, Delta+, Zeta, and Alpha.
